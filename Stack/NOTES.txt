Stack: Last IN First OUT
FYI: We are implementing stack using singly linked list unshift and shift operations.
FYI: In the linked list adding to end and removing from end is expensive operation thats why we are doing this by shift and unshift
5->4->3->2->1
after pop
4->3->2->1

---------- Complexity--------
Insertion: O(1)
Removal: O(1)
Searching: O(N)
Access: O(N)

1. Undo/Redo
